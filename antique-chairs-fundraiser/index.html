Our Mission

Every antique chair has a story to tell. From Victorian parlor chairs to Arts & Crafts movement pieces, these treasures represent centuries of skilled craftsmanship and cultural heritage.

Our fundraising campaign aims to:

  • Restore damaged pieces to their original glory
  • Preserve chairs for future generations
  • Create educational displays about furniture history
  • Share these beautiful pieces with the community
